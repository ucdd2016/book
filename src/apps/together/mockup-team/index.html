WHAT IS MUSIC TOGETHER

Music_Together allows people to easily collaborate and make music together! Finally, users can now use the power of numbers to write scores never imagined!

GET CREATIVE

Music Together's mission to help your creativity flourish. Users will be able to set musical scores, curate their own tempos, and share their collective masterpieces. Let your imagination run wild with Music Together!

WORK TOGETHER

Music Together is all about putting the best of people's musical talent together to create a masterpiece. Work with others, share and benefit from the knowledge of others. Music Together allows large groups to create single pieces of music.
